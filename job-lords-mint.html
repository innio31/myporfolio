📋 Role Overview

At Lord's Mint, I managed sales operations and data systems for a growing manufacturing company. This foundational role taught me the critical relationship between accurate data and business success. I was responsible for maintaining sales pipelines, generating reports for management, and implementing systems that improved operational efficiency.

This experience was instrumental in shaping my analytical mindset and understanding of how technology can transform business operations — lessons I continue to apply in my software development work today.

🎯 Key Responsibilities

  • Sales Data Management: Maintained accurate records of all sales transactions, customer information, and inventory levels using Excel and manual ledger systems.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Generated daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports for management review, identifying trends and areas for improvement.
  • Process Improvement: Streamlined data entry workflows by creating standardized templates and validation rules that reduced errors and processing time.
  • Inventory Coordination: Collaborated with the sales team to track inventory levels and flag reorder points, reducing stockout incidents.
  • Customer Record Management: Maintained customer databases and purchase histories to support relationship management and targeted sales efforts.
  • Team Collaboration: Worked closely with the sales team to ensure data integrity and resolve discrepancies in transaction records.
